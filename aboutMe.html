My main aspirations of the course are to complete the degree with hopefully a 2.1 or ideally a first, giving me the best chances of finding employment and starting a career in an area that I love and enjoy.

As things stand (Semester 1) the majority of my assignments and submissions have been of the upper boundaries of marks, with the main assignments having a mark of 85 and 65 out of 100 (a 1st and 2.1) which is showing a good standing for the first year so far, and I'm hoping this will continue throughout the rest of the year and onwards.
