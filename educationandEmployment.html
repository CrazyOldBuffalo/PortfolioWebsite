Below is a list of all jobs I have worked including skills gained and dates.

Bar Staff (20th July 2019 - Current)

Part time bar staff for the Venue (Stocksbridge) serving soft and alcoholic drinks to customers. Work involved a large amount of teamwork to ensure people weren’t waiting too long for their drinks whilst also being able to process payments using cash registers and cards. Whilst working I always try to have a positive attitude and joke around with my colleagues to make the shift more enjoyable for everyone and help it to pass by faster. I'm working part time here to help subsidise my studies and support my hobbies and interest whilst at university, helping overall with my financial situation.

Assistant IT Technician at DXC Technologies (June 2016 - December 2017)

I worked at DXC as part of my Level 3 Apprenticeship which I successfully completed in July of this year. I fulfilled two roles during my time at DXC, the first was as a 1st line Help-desk technician answering phones from users that were having issues with their equipment or software. Most of this job involved me filling in electronic tickets using the companies ticketing system for them to be passed on to a further support team as well as performing troubleshooting and resolution activities before they are passed on. As a secondary role I also worked as an administrator managing tickets by sending them to support staff and keeping their workloads full, whilst also dealing with management requests by communicating with them and creating request tickets.

Match Day Staff (12th - 26th August 2014)

Worked part time for Sheffield United as Match Day Staff serving drinks and food for customers. Teamwork and communication skills were essential for the role especially during half-time when there would be a rush, I was required to be able to handle the pressure of serving as many people as possible in a small space of time.

IT Department at Sheffield Futures (12th - 26th August 2014) Volunteering work

I helped move large quantities of old computers from one location to the other and removed the hard drives from these whilst stacking them in corresponding piles and helped to sort out their storeroom ready for the computers to be put into it. This involved me to be very hardworking and to be committed as there was nearly 200 computers that all had to be transported and sorted into piles.
